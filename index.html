About Us

LUXE Certification is an independent Certification Body established as per requirement of IS/ISO/IEC 17021-1:2015 (Conformity Assessment-Requirements for Bodies providing Audit and Certification of Management Systems). LUXE Certification is fast growing management Certification body in India. Luxe Certification is accredited by UG-AC & Provide Certificate for ISO 9001, ISO 14001, OHSAS 18001, ISO 20000, ISO 27001, ISO 50001, ISO 13485 and many More.
